Rules & Regulations

1. Eligibility

All players must be at least 18 years of age and registered with The Amatour. A valid GHIN (Golf Handicap Index Number) is required to ensure fair net scoring across events.

2. Scoring Format

  • All events are individual stroke play unless otherwise noted.
  • Gross and Net scores are recorded for every round.
  • Net scores are calculated using course-adjusted handicaps.
  • Scorecards must be submitted immediately following your round to be counted.

3. Points System

Points are awarded for participation and performance:

  • Finale Points contribute to end-of-season standings and the Championship Invite.
  • Purchase Points can be redeemed toward raffle entries or discounts on future events.
  • Winners (Net and Gross) earn additional points.
  • Early registration bonuses may apply.

4. Tees and Course Ratings

Tees are assigned by gender unless otherwise communicated. Course and slope ratings, along with yardages, are used to fairly determine net scores across diverse playing fields.

5. Groupings & Tee Times

Tee groups are published prior to each event and are subject to change. Players are expected to arrive early and be on the tee box at least 10 minutes before their scheduled time.

6. Code of Conduct

  • Respect your fellow players and the course at all times.
  • Abusive language, cheating, or unsportsmanlike conduct will result in disqualification.
  • Be pace-aware—keep up with the group ahead.

7. Registration & Cancellations

  • All participants must register prior to the registration deadline.
  • No-shows or cancellations within 48 hours of an event may forfeit entry fees or points.

8. Ties

Ties for placement or points will be broken by scorecard playoff starting from the #1 handicap hole.

9. Rule Enforcement

The Amatour committee reserves the right to enforce rules, make rulings on disputes, and disqualify players as necessary. All decisions are final.

10. Event Rules & Regulations

Scoring & Gameplay

  • Lost or Errant Shots: +1 stroke penalty with drop on line of entry.
  • Out of Bounds (White Stakes): +2 stroke penalty with drop on line of entry, or +1 stroke with re-tee.
  • Divots: Declared unplayable with +1 stroke penalty.
  • Man-Made Structures: Free relief within 1 club length, no closer to hole.
  • Gimmies: Not allowed — all putts must be holed out.
  • Score Cap: Max score per hole is double par.

Scoring & Submission

  • 1 scorecard per 2 players (2 per foursome); 1 for groups of 3.
  • Write total scores for each hole.
  • Makeup rounds: take photo of scorecard and send to Alex.
  • Submit scores by handing in card or texting a clear photo to Alex.

Closest to the Pin (KP)

  • Use tape measurers and sheet near the green.
  • Measuring must take ≤30 seconds with 2 people.
  • Makeup rounds are not eligible for KP prizes.

Pace of Play

  • Stay within 1 hole of the group ahead.
  • Be efficient with drops and watch the line of entry.

Payouts

  • KP Prizes: $10 each.
  • Net Winner: Payout varies by participation.

Makeups

  • Makeups not eligible for KPs.
  • Must declare tee time/date in advance.
  • Photo of scorecard with attester is required.
  • Max 3 makeup rounds per season.

Earning Purchase Points

  • 1st: 15 points, 2nd: 8 points, 3rd: 5 points.

Redeeming Purchase Points

  • 10 points = 1 Raffle Ticket.
  • 3 points = $1 Event Credit.
  • Can be banked and redeemed anytime.

Earning Finale Points (Net Score Only)

  • 1st: 160, 2nd: 140, 3rd: 130, 4th: 120, 5th: 110, 6th: 100
  • ... 10th: 60, 11th and below: 50

Finale Points Advantage

  • 1st Overall: Starts at -3 strokes
  • 2nd–5th Overall: Start at -2 strokes
  • 6th–10th Overall: Start at -1 stroke
  • Applies to Net scoring only in the Finale

General

  • Be kind, be honest, and play your best.
  • Use best judgment and group consensus when unclear.
  • Missed tee time = +1 stroke over last-place net score to earn Finale Points.
