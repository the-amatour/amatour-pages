Rules & Regulations

1. Eligibility

All players must be at least 18 years of age and registered with The Amatour. A valid GHIN (Golf Handicap Index Number) is required to ensure fair net scoring across events.

2. Scoring Format

  • All events are individual stroke play unless otherwise noted.
  • Gross and Net scores are recorded for every round.
  • Net scores are calculated using course-adjusted handicaps.
  • Scorecards must be submitted immediately following your round to be counted.

3. Points System

Points are awarded for participation and performance:

  • Finale Points contribute to end-of-season standings and the Championship Invite.
  • Purchase Points can be redeemed toward raffle entries or discounts on future events.
  • Winners (Net and Gross) earn additional points.
  • Early registration bonuses may apply.

4. Tees and Course Ratings

Tees are assigned by gender unless otherwise communicated. Course and slope ratings, along with yardages, are used to fairly determine net scores across diverse playing fields.

5. Groupings & Tee Times

Tee groups are published prior to each event and are subject to change. Players are expected to arrive early and be on the tee box at least 10 minutes before their scheduled time.

6. Code of Conduct

  • Respect your fellow players and the course at all times.
  • Abusive language, cheating, or unsportsmanlike conduct will result in disqualification.

7. Registration & Cancellations

  • All participants must register prior to the registration deadline.
  • No-shows or cancellations within 48 hours of an event may forfeit entry fees or points.

8. Ties

Ties for placement or points will be broken by scorecard playoff starting from the #1 handicap hole.

9. Rule Enforcement

The Amatour committee reserves the right to enforce rules, make rulings on disputes, and disqualify players as necessary. All decisions are final.
