1. Player & Event Registration

Administrative requirements for joining the league and registering for individual events.

Rule Details
Eligibility All players must track their scores on a public golf application (e.g., 18Birdies or TheGrint).
Registration All participants must register prior to the registration deadline for each event, registration covers entry fees and green fees.
Cancellations & No-Shows A missed tee time (no-show or last minute cancellation) will result in a +1 stroke penalty over the last-place net score to earn Finale Points.
Refunds Registration entry fees are nonrefundable, green fees can only be transferred if the player can find a replacement.
Groupings & Tee Times Tee groups are published prior to each event. Players are expected to arrive early and be on the tee box at least 30 minutes before their scheduled time.
Missed Tee Time Penalty A missed tee time results in a +1 stroke penalty over the last-place net score to earn Finale Points and full registration fee will not be refunded.

2. On-Course Gameplay Rules

Specific local course rules, penalties, and essential conduct for playing a round in the league.

Rule Category Details
Format / Tees All events are individual stroke play unless otherwise noted. Tees are assigned by gender unless otherwise communicated.
Gimmies Not allowed — all putts must be holed out.
Score Cap Maximum score per hole is double par.
Lost or Errant Shots +1 stroke penalty with drop on the line of entry.
Out of Bounds +2 stroke penalty with drop on the line of entry, OR +1 stroke with a re-tee.
Divots (Unplayable Lie) Declared unplayable with +1 stroke penalty, player still has the choice to play it as it lies.
Man-Made Structures Free relief within 1 club length, no closer to the hole.
Rule Discretion Use best judgment and group consensus when rules are unclear.
Range Finders Usage of slope on range finders are permitted unless otherwise stated.

3. Pace of Play & Conduct

Standards for maintaining appropriate pace and professional conduct during league events.

Pace Requirements

  • Stay within 1 hole of the group ahead
  • Be pace-aware and keep up with the group ahead
  • Be efficient with drops and watch the line of entry

Code of Conduct

  • Respect your fellow players and the course at all times
  • Abusive language, cheating, or unsportsmanlike conduct will result in disqualification

Rule Enforcement

The Amatour committee reserves the right to enforce rules, make rulings on disputes, and disqualify players. All decisions are final.

4. Scoring & Submission

Guidelines for score calculation, scorecard handling, and submission procedures.

A. Score Calculation & Requirements

  • Gross and Net scores are recorded for every round
  • Net scores are calculated using course-adjusted handicaps
  • Course and slope ratings, along with yardages, are used to fairly determine net scores

B. Scorecard Protocol

Card Requirements 1 scorecard per 2 players (2 per foursome); 1 for groups of 3.
Recording Write total scores for each hole.
Submission Deadline Scorecards must be submitted immediately following your round to be counted. Submit by handing in the card or texting a clear photo to Alex.
Ties Ties for placement or points will be broken by scorecard playoff starting from the #1 handicap hole.

C. Closest to the Pin (KP)

Use tape measures and the sheet near the green. Measuring must take ≤30 seconds with 2 people.
