What is The Amatour?

The Amatour is a competitive and social golf league built for players of all skill levels. With a season-long points system, end-of-season finale, raffles, and winners for each event, we blend camaraderie with competition.

⛳ Mixer Scramble

Team play with bonus entry perks — designed as a meet & greet with a side of big swings.

🏆 Points System

Earn points for placements, streaks, and more — a FedEx Cup adaptation.

🎟️ Raffle Rewards

Participate and win big with raffle entries tied to your engagement and performance.
