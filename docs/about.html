What is The Amatour?

The Amatour is a golf league built for everyday players who love the game and the grind. As golfers, we invest time and effort in our craft and this is a place to showcase your hard work. Whether you're chasing low scores, new friendships, or want rounds on your calendar with no work, The Amatour is where casual meets competitive.
